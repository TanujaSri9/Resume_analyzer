Frequently Asked Questions

Is my resume safe? Will it be stored?

Your resume is deleted immediately after we extract text for analysis. We never store the actual file. The analysis results are saved privately in your browser session only.

What is an ATS score and why does it matter?

ATS stands for Applicant Tracking System — the software most companies use to filter resumes before a human ever sees them. An ATS score indicates how well your resume passes this automated filter. A score below 60% means your resume is likely being rejected automatically.

What file formats are supported?

We support PDF{% if docx_supported %} and Word (.docx){% endif %} files up to 8 MB. Please ensure your resume contains actual text (not just a scanned image). A text-based PDF works best.

Is this completely free?

Yes — 100% free. No credit card, no subscription, no signup. Just upload and analyze.

How accurate is the ATS score?

The score is generated by AI and is designed to give you a directionally accurate estimate of your resume's ATS performance. It's excellent for identifying weak areas and gaps. While no tool can perfectly replicate every ATS system, consistently scoring above 75% significantly improves your chances.

What is the Mock Interview feature?

Mock Interview is an AI-powered chat simulator where an AI interviewer asks you 5 role-specific questions (mix of HR and technical), evaluates your answers in real time, gives constructive feedback after each answer, and provides a final performance summary with a score out of 10.

Can I analyze multiple resumes?

Yes! You can analyze as many resumes as you like (up to 5 per hour to prevent abuse). All your reports are saved in your private history so you can compare different versions.
